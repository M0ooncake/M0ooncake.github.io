Pitch


Alice in Public Domain is an action-adventure, beat 'em up-style game similar to Super Mario Galaxy, with the character switching and comboing of Genshin Impact. Players will be playing as Alice and a cast of other public domain characters as they face down the mysterious Foe that has infected the public domain


Description

Experience an unprecedented adventure where public domain favourites like Alice and Pooh come together for the first time, each bringing their own worlds, challenges, and stories into an interconnected narrative!

Switch between iconic characters, each with distinct abilities and playstyles, and master unique strategies to counter enemies with their own adaptive powers. No two encounters are the same, making every battle a tactical challenge


Unique Selling Points

  • Victorian storybook art style with a harmonious mix of 2D and 3D elements
  • Characters and artworks from the public domain
  • Character switching
  • Puzzles that require using both characters
  • Fun Combat system
  • Entertaining narrative with satirical elements

History
