It Is Now In a Pretty Good State.

Published: 19/11/2024 -|- Last Edited: Never

  Over the past few days I have made big strides to get this website closer to being launch ready. Sometimes it can be hard to find the motivation or discipline to get at it and actually get things done. However, I have gotten quite the kick of energy and focus with new medication I am currently taking. It has been very exciting to see this start taking shape.

 

  I had taken a break from HTML and CSS for a while. But it has been a fun time familiarizing myself with the language again. Seeing interesting things you can do, different animations that can be pulled off and such. Perhaps the current colours may be a bit much, in which case I can look into changing that later. The current look pops quite a bit, and may not look the best in a professional setting.

Domain Name

  Currently as it stands, the domain name is quite wack. Congratulations if you somehow found this page anyway without being given the link! I would very much like to acquire a proper domain for this site. But only once I feel I have gotten this site into a near complete state.

Future of The Site

  I am not yet sure where exactly I want to take this as a site. I am currently enjoying myself just messing around and seeing what works and what doesn't. Though that is also quite applicable to almost any development situation. It is often enjoyable running around in a sandbox and making whatever you can think of. That sort of tinkering and problem-solving is what got me into game development in the first place.
  Which currently is a ways off. I need to design a press kit page with all the right fixings and entries needing. Though, I suppose there is not much yet to enter into such a page. But having the backbone of it would be fantastic. What really needs to happen is getting the pages for the projects in. The portfolio pieces need to be completed. Once the first two are done, work can begin other pieces.
